<div align="center">
  <h1>Lars mod</h1>
  <p>
    A small mod created for a friend that uses the demo mod as a base and some tidbits of the Soggins mod for dialogue handling for the Cobalt Core game.
  </p>
  <p>
    This mod has three characters, a vaporeon called Aether, whose whole gimmick is being able to use aqua ring on your own ship, and it synergizes with heat and acid/oxidation on enemy ships.
    Then we have a Charmander, their main focus is applying heat to the enemy, although they also get a decent amount of movement.
    And then we have a serperior, they are capable of inflicting oxidation on the enemy, which evolves into acid, as well as multiple midrow items that can do a variety of things
  </p>
